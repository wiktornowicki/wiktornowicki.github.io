About the creator

Hey, I'm Wiktor! I'm an n/b programming student from Poland, mainly focusing on web designing and GUI app designing. I am pretty much an amateur at this point, however I don't think I'll ever be more than that. I'm doing everything purely for fun, and of course, to help other people out. I'm trying to create my own programming language, and I suppose it'll take a while, but hey! It'll happen some day. My hobbies are pretty much nothing but playing RPGs/rhythm-based games, playing guitar and programming. Oh, yeah, and I make VMs.

I'm doing my best to improve at these things, and I guess I'm getting somewhere. I mean, I made this page without using any external templates.

If you find me on some crappy forum making fun of people or see me begging for VESA drivers for ancient Windows versions on BetaArchive, don't be surprised. It's a lifestyle. Well, that's about it for talking about me.

I also am working on my own Linux distribution, which I codenamed "Prism Linux".
